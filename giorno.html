About

- The Honda Giorno is a retro-styled scooter that combines classic aesthetics with modern features. Available in two variants—Giorno Standard and Giorno+—it caters to different riding preferences and markets.

- 125cc, liquid-cooled, 4-valve, single-cylinder, SOHC eSP+

- Top Speed: Suitable for city and highway use

- LED headlamp and tail lamp

- Digital instrument panel

- The Honda Giorno series offers a blend of classic design and modern functionality. While the Standard model is perfect for those seeking a lightweight, economical scooter, the Giorno+ provides enhanced performance and features for a more versatile riding experience. Both models maintain Honda's reputation for reliability and quality.
